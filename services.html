Bookkeeping

Day-to-day numbers, in plain English, on a schedule that suits.

Book a bookkeeping chat

The boring-on-purpose layer that keeps everything else working. We reconcile your transactions, lodge your BAS, and send you a one-page monthly summary that's actually readable.

Most clients are sole traders, tradies, or small companies with 50–500 transactions a month. If you're newer than that, the Starter tier covers you; if you're bigger, we'll scale up.

What's included

  • Xero · MYOB · QuickBooks set-up & training. We're certified in all three and have no commission deal with any.
  • Monthly bank reconciliation. All transactions categorised, errors flagged, anything weird raised before EOFY.
  • BAS lodgement & GST reporting. Quarterly or monthly. Lodged on time. Receipt sent.
  • Accounts payable / receivable. Bills paid, invoices chased, cashflow visible.
  • End-of-month financial summaries. One page. Plain English. The numbers that actually matter.

How it goes

01

First-month handover

Connect your bank feed and any existing software. Usually one week.

02

Monthly reconciliation

We do the work. You get a summary email on the first of the next month.

03

Quarterly BAS

Lodged on your behalf. Receipts and a one-line explanation of any unusual line.

02

Taxation

Returns and lodgements — individuals, sole traders, companies.

Book a tax appointment

The annual return is the visible bit. Behind it: tax planning, CGT advice, ATO correspondence, and the discipline of doing it right the first time so the ATO doesn't come back with questions.

We handle straightforward PAYG returns, sole-trader and partnership returns, company and trust tax, and the messy multi-source years (multiple jobs, side income, share sales).

What's included

  • Individual tax returns. PAYG, multiple jobs, side income, work-from-home claims, etc.
  • Sole-trader & partnership returns. Schedules done, deductions identified.
  • Company & trust tax. Including franking accounts and dividend statements.
  • Capital Gains Tax advice. Property, shares, business sales — before, not after.
  • Tax planning. Before EOFY, not in October. We'll tell you what to do in March, not what you should have done.
  • ATO correspondence. Letters, audits, payment plans — handled directly. You don't have to call them.

How it goes

01

Send what you have

Group certificates, invoices, receipts — even a folder of photos works.

02

We prepare

Draft return back within a week. You read it. Ask anything.

03

Lodge & done

Lodged with the ATO. Refund or bill, you'll know in writing.

Payroll & Compliance

STP, super, leave — handled without the headache.

Book a payroll chat

The moment you hire your first employee, the rulebook doubles. We run payroll, lodge STP, track super and leave, and make sure the right amounts hit the right places at the right time.

Best for small businesses with 1–20 staff. Bigger? We can still help — or refer you to a specialist we trust.

What's included

  • Single Touch Payroll (STP) reporting, on time, every pay run.
  • Super lodgement & SGC compliance. Quarterly, never late.
  • Leave entitlement tracking. Annual leave, sick leave, long service — accrued and reconciled.
  • WorkCover, payroll tax, employee onboarding. The non-glamorous compliance bits.
  • FBT & salary-packaging advice. Whether the car / phone / laptop arrangement is actually saving anyone money.

How it goes

01

Set up

We import your existing employee data, audit the awards, fix any old rates.

02

Run payroll

Weekly, fortnightly, or monthly. STP lodged automatically.

03

Quarterly close

Super, payroll tax, WorkCover — settled and reconciled.

Business Advisory

Strategy, cashflow, and structure for growing operations.

Book an advisory chat

The "what should we actually do next?" conversations. We look at your numbers, talk through where the business is going, and translate that into structure, cashflow, and decisions.

Most useful when revenue is growing, when you're thinking about hiring, when you're considering a new entity, or when something just feels wrong.

What's included

  • Cashflow forecasting. 6 / 12 / 24-month view. Updated monthly.
  • Business structure. Sole trader / partnership / company / trust — which suits, when to change.
  • Monthly management reporting. Profit, cashflow, key ratios. Explained.
  • Profit & Loss analysis. What's actually driving the numbers, what to fix first.
  • Succession & exit planning. If you might sell or hand over in the next 3–5 years, plan it now.

How it goes

Discovery session

02

Plan + numbers

03

Quarterly check-in

Are we on track? What changed? What's next?

05

SMSF & Property

Self-managed super and investment-property accounting.

Book a property / SMSF chat

The two areas where doing it wrong is most expensive. Self-managed super funds carry strict compliance obligations; investment property accounting carries strict depreciation and CGT rules. Both reward thinking ahead.

Best for people who already have an SMSF or an investment property and want a steady hand on the paperwork — or who are about to set one up and want to do it right.

What's included

  • SMSF set-up & annual compliance. Trust deed review, ATO registration, ongoing audit-ready records.
  • SMSF tax returns & member statements. Annual filing, contribution caps, pension phase if applicable.
  • Investment property income & expenses. Rent, agent fees, repairs vs. improvements — categorised correctly.
  • Depreciation schedules. Capital works + plant & equipment, prepared with a QS where needed.
  • Capital Gains on property sales. Planned ahead of sale, not after.

How it goes

01

Document gather

Trust deed, statements, lease agreements, depreciation schedules.

02

Annual return

SMSF return or property schedule prepared, member balances reconciled.

03

Plan next year

Contribution strategy or property decisions — discussed in advance.
