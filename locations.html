Working online

Bookkeeping doesn't need a postcode.

About half of Bodagh's clients are entirely online. We onboard you without a face-to-face, you send documents via secure upload, we meet over Zoom when we need to talk — and most months we don't need to.

If you're outside Northern Melbourne and worried that means we can't help: it doesn't. Most of what we do is the same regardless of which side of the Yarra you're on.

  • Secure document upload for everything tax-related — no email-attaching tax file numbers.
  • Zoom meetings in any time zone. Most fit into a 30-minute window.
  • Same-day email replies during business hours. Same as our walk-ins.
  • Digital signing on engagement letters and tax returns. No printers.
  • Xero / MYOB / QuickBooks — your data, you keep access, we just plug in.
