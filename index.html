How it works

Three steps. One chat to start.

Most people are surprised how light the process feels. No paperwork before you've met us, no jargon during, and a clear summary after.

  1. 01

    Book a 30-min chat

    Pick a time online. No paperwork yet — just a chat about where you're at and what you need.

    Free. No pitch. No commitment. By the end you'll know what we'd actually do for you and roughly what it'd cost.

  2. 02

    We get you set up

    We'll tell you exactly what to send (less than you think), and handle the rest in Xero, MYOB, or QuickBooks.

    Usually a one-week handover. We'll take over from your previous accountant directly if you'd rather not have that conversation.

  3. 03

    Ongoing, in plain English

    Monthly check-ins. BAS lodged on time. A summary that actually makes sense. You always know your numbers.

    Reach Lara by SMS, email, or just walk in. Most questions get a same-day reply.
